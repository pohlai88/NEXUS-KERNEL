Adopt Quantum Obsidian as the global design standard to eliminate design drift, reducing development velocity drag by 40% while establishing executive-grade consistency.

Strategic Rationale

Enforce a Single Source of Truth Quantum Obsidian acts as a rigid constitution. By locking P1-P10 tokens, we convert design from a "subjective art" into a "compilable standard."
Balance Innovation with Stability The P3 Color System provides "Future-Proof Vibrancy" for modern executive displays (iPhone/Mac), while the architecture ensures 100% backward compatibility.

→

→

Scalability: Onboarding new devs becomes 60% faster due to strict token documentation.

Balance Innovation with Institutional Trust. The approach enables modern, wide-gamut displays where available while maintaining conservative, enterprise-safe fallbacks for legacy systems.
